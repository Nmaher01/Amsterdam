Air

Located in the very centre of Amsterdam near Rembrandtplein, AIR is a nightclub designed by Dutchman Marcel Wanders. The club is split into multiple rooms and can hold up to 1300 people, with five bars and lockers to provide guests with a more secure option than coat check. It's equipped with a Void Acoustics soundsystem and features walls lined with lights and a video projection screen. The nightclub also features the unique Air Card, which is used for payment for drinks at the bar.
