Winston Kingdom

The Winston Kingdom is a venue for live bands, poetry, dance nights and parties. The evening styles can be very diverse, ranging from punk to alternative, from live toasting MC’s to a simple Tiësto style dance night. As diverse as the style is, so is the audience, you can be sure that they are in a good mood. Usually it’s quite crowded, because next to the folks that are there especially for the event, there are lots of people just dropping by, that’s because the Winston is right in the center of Amsterdam’s nightlife.
