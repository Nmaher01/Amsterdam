Club ABE

With the opening of ABE Amsterdam 30 somethings can enjoy a night out in style in this new club and lounge at the Amstelstraat 32. ABE Amsterdam is located in a formerbanking building that dates back from 1871. Details in the interior design remind of this former use. Expect a lot of gold and money used as decoration combined with classic details and a pink and green touch. The name of the club is the nickname of the banker who once build the house.
