Club 8

Yes, sounds like a weird combination. And actually it is. But who cares about that? Club 8 is a place where you can play pool and discover the music you will be listening to years from now. The place might look a little cheap, but so are the drinks. If you don't mind graffiti on the walls, Club 8 has more than one reason to visit. The first floor has 19 pool and 3 snooker tables. The top floor in a spacious club. If you feel like a great 'underground' party you might want to try Club 8.
