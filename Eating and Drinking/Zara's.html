Zara's

When Susie Roche opened Zaza's in 2003, the restaurant's theme revolved around the idea that people should enjoy life and have fun, which is reflected in the food and the atmosphere. The menu is composed of delicious dishes featuring influences from France, The Mediterranean and Asia. Start with pan-fried scallops or the carrot and ginger soup. For a main course, the fillet of sea wolf or venison will make your mouth water. Craving something to break-up the meal? Try an in between course, such as the half portion of homemade ravioli or lemon flavored sorbet. Finish the night with a slice of lemon meringue pie or white chocolate panna cotta. To top it off, the menu changes every three months, so you'll always find something new and delightful.
