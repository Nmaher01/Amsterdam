't Arendsnest Beer Bar

Established 5 years ago, Peter designed this 'timeless' bar himself, combining old and modern features to create a warm atmosphere. Above the traditional wooden bar and stools hang old ship lamps, that along with the chandeliers are unique to the interior.

't Arendsnest sell 150 different types of seasonal and regular bottled Beer, with 15 on draft. Their policy is that they only sell Dutch Beer, with at least one from each of the 52 Breweries in Holland.
